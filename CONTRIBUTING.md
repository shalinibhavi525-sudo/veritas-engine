# Contributing to Veritas Engine

Thank you for your interest in improving Veritas! As an independent research project, I welcome contributions that help democratize AI safety.

## How to Contribute
1. **Report Bugs:** Open an issue describing the bug and your environment.
2. **Feature Requests:** Suggest new optimization techniques (e.g., for mobile/ARM).
3. **Pull Requests:** 
    - Fork the repo.
    - Create a feature branch.
    - Ensure your code follows the existing style.
    - Submit the PR for review.

## Community Guidelines
Please be respectful and constructive in all interactions. Our goal is to make AI safety tools accessible to everyone, regardless of their hardware or location.
